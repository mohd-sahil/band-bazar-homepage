About Bandbazar

Bandbazaar.com is an online platform that provides marketing tools and industry opportunities for emerging musicians, artists, bands, event manager’s photographers, graphic designers, sound engineers, and band crew members to build their careers. We’ve connected artists to venues, festivals, brands, publishers, labels, and the fans themselves. Bandbazaar.com provide a social networking platform for bands to connect to their community
